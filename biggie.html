Lucky To Have Found You

How I Met My Cat Biggie

I'll never forget this moment, it was early in the morning. I was annoyed becuase my sister had asked me to take her cat in for a checkup at the vet. Normally I would'nt have been annoyed, but it was my first day off from work, little did I know I would meet Biggie.

I dropped off my sisters cat and immediatley went over to the cat adoption area at PetSmart. I remember putting my hand up to the glass and him rubbing his face up against it as if I were petting him.
